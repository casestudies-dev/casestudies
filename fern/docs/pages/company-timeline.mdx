# Company Timeline

`GET /v1/companies/{slug}/timeline`

View case studies in chronological order with industry breakdown. Useful for tracking growth during due diligence.

## Example

```bash
curl "https://whatsapp-backend-knd2.onrender.com/v1/companies/checkout/timeline" \
  -H "X-API-Key: YOUR_KEY"
```

## What to look for

- **Growing case study count** — signals active customer success investment
- **New industries appearing** — signals market expansion
- **Consistent publishing** — signals healthy customer pipeline
